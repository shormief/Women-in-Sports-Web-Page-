How Many Can You Recognize?

How Many Can You Recognize (pt.2)?

Why Does This Matter?

There are currently 12 WNBA teams can you name any. You probably can’t but don’t worry I couldn’t either. As a person who enjoys watching basketball, I find it sad that I know nothing about the WNBA. I could name at least 6 NBA teams off the top of my head and that’s half of the league of women’s teams yet not one of the teams or logos that I saw was familiar.

The problem starts before college and before getting drafted. According to Ackerman Report, more girls participate in high school basketball than any other sport very few have interest or dreams of making it to the WNBA or even as far as college basketball. Maybe these girls have been disheartened by the fact that even if they were the MVP, their salary would be a little more than a fifth of the league minimum at the NBA level. Now although the WNBA has been around for 16 years and the NBA has been around for 66 years, the gap in pay and media coverage is huge. The WNBA hasn’t even broken $1 million cap whilst this year Kobe Bryant signed a $100 million deal with Golden State Warriors.

These women work just as hard as their male counterparts don’t see many results and as a result some are not even trying for the WNBA. Then there are those who do finally make it but leave for lack of money or go to international teams that supply them with greater offers and chances.
